What Are Cosmetic Veneers?

Porcelain veneers are thin, custom-made shells that cover the front surface of your teeth. They're designed to improve the appearance of teeth that are stained, chipped, misaligned, or of unequal size.

Why Choose Veneers?

Veneers are a perfect solution for patients who want to enhance their smile without undergoing extensive orthodontic treatment or complex restorations.

Ideal for:

  • ✓ Discolored or stained teeth
  • ✓ Slightly chipped or worn teeth
  • ✓ Gaps between teeth
  • ✓ Misaligned or uneven teeth
  • ✓ Wanting a brighter smile
  • ✓ Cosmetic smile enhancement

The Veneer Process

Our streamlined process ensures beautiful, natural results with minimal disruption to your daily life.

Visit 1: Consultation & Design

We discuss your smile goals and examine your teeth. Using digital smile design technology, we preview exactly how your new veneers will look before any work begins.

Visit 2: Preparation & Shade

Minimal tooth preparation (0.5mm removal). We take precise shade and shape measurements, then send your specifications to our lab for custom veneer fabrication.

Visit 3: Placement & Bonding

Your custom veneers are carefully bonded to your teeth using advanced adhesive techniques. Final adjustments ensure perfect fit, comfort, and appearance.
